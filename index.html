A Taste of the World –
Experience Dishes from Every Continent

American

American food is diverse, ranging from burgers and barbecue to tex-mex and soul food. Fast food is popular, but regional dishes like jambalaya also stand out.

Asian

Asian food is rich in flavors and varies by region, from sushi in Japan to curry in India. Common ingredients include rice, noodles, soy sauce, and a balance of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy tastes.

Brazilian

Brazilian food is diverse, featuring dishes like feijoada, a black bean stew with pork, and pão de queijo, cheesy bread rolls. It blends Indigenous, African, and Portuguese influences, often using ingredients like cassava, rice, and tropical fruits.

Greek

Greek food is known for its fresh ingredients, featuring dishes like moussaka, souvlaki, and tzatziki. Olive oil, feta cheese, herbs, and grilled meats play a central role in its Mediterranean flavors.

Indian

Indian food is rich in spices and flavors, with dishes like curry, biryani, and samosas. It varies by region, using ingredients like lentils, rice, and naan, often flavored with turmeric, cumin, and cardamom.

Italian

Italian food is famous for its simplicity and fresh ingredients, with dishes like pasta, pizza, and risotto. Olive oil, tomatoes, basil, and cheese are essential in its Mediterranean flavors.

Japanese

Japanese food is known for its balance and presentation, featuring dishes like sushi, ramen, and tempura. Common ingredients include rice, seafood, soy sauce, and umami-rich flavors.

Korean

Korean food is bold and flavorful, with dishes like kimchi, bibimbap, and bulgogi. It often features fermented ingredients, spicy gochujang, and a variety of side dishes called banchan.

Lebanese

Lebanese food is known for its fresh ingredients and vibrant flavors, with dishes like hummus, tabbouleh, and shawarma. Olive oil, garlic, and herbs are commonly used, along with a variety of grains, vegetables, and grilled meats.

Mediterranean

Mediterranean food is celebrated for its healthy ingredients, including olive oil, fresh vegetables, fish, and whole grains. Dishes like Greek salad, falafel, and paella highlight the region’s emphasis on bold flavors and simple preparation.

Mexican

Mexican food is vibrant and full of flavor, with dishes like tacos, enchiladas, and guacamole. It often features ingredients like corn, beans, chili peppers, and fresh herbs, creating a perfect balance of spicy and savory.

Moroccan

Moroccan food is known for its aromatic spices and rich flavors, with dishes like tagine, couscous, and pastilla. It combines ingredients such as lamb, chicken, dried fruits, and a variety of spices like cumin, cinnamon, and saffron.

Pakistani

Pakistani food is known for its rich, bold flavors, with dishes like biryani, kebabs, and nihari. It often incorporates spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala, along with ingredients like rice, lentils, and yogurt.

Russian

Russian food is hearty and comforting, with dishes like borscht, pelmeni, and beef stroganoff. It often features ingredients like potatoes, cabbage, mushrooms, and sour cream, reflecting the country’s cold climate and agricultural roots.

Smoothie

Smoothie bowls are a healthy and refreshing meal option, often made with blended fruits, yogurt, and toppings like granola, nuts, and seeds. They offer a balance of vitamins, fiber, and protein, making them a popular choice for breakfast or a light snack.

Thai

Thai food is known for its bold and balanced flavors, with dishes like pad Thai, green curry, and tom yum soup. It combines spicy, sweet, sour, and salty elements using ingredients like lemongrass, chili, coconut milk, and fish sauce.

Turkish

Turkish food is rich in flavor and tradition, with dishes like kebabs, mezes, and baklava. It features ingredients like lamb, yogurt, olive oil, and fresh vegetables, often prepared with aromatic spices such as cumin and sumac.
